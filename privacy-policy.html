Qibla Time Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last Updated: June 26, 2024

1. Information We Collect

A. Location Data

We collect your location data only when you use the app to:

  • Calculate accurate prayer times based on your current position
  • Determine the correct Qibla direction

Important: Your location data is processed locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

B. Device Sensors

The app requires these sensors to function:

  • Accelerometer - For device orientation
  • Magnetometer (compass) - For Qibla direction
Note: Devices without these sensors cannot install the app from Google Play.

C. Notification Preferences

Your prayer notification settings are stored locally on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

A. Prayer Time Data

We use the Aladhan API to fetch prayer times. Your location coordinates are sent to this service only to get prayer times for your location.

B. Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob may collect anonymous data according to Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Security & Retention

  • All processing is local - No data leaves your device
  • No user accounts - We don't collect personal information
  • Location data - Used temporarily and discarded
  • Notification settings - Stored only on your device
