Qibla Time Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 16, 2025

Last Updated: July 16, 2025

1. Information Collection and Use

Location Data

The app collects your precise location (GPS) to:

  • Calculate accurate Qibla direction
  • Determine prayer times for your location
  • Show distance to Kaaba
Important: Location data is processed locally and never stored on our servers.

Device Sensors

The app requires:

  • Accelerometer - For compass functionality
  • Magnetometer - For Qibla direction calculation

2. Permissions

Permission Usage
ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION For precise Qibla direction and prayer times
ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION Fallback when precise location unavailable
VIBRATE Haptic feedback when facing Qibla
INTERNET Fetch prayer times from Aladhan API
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Check internet connectivity
WAKE_LOCK Keep screen on during Qibla use
POST_NOTIFICATIONS Show prayer time notifications
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED Restart prayer alarms after device reboot

3. Data Sharing

We share limited information with:

  • Aladhan API - Only your coordinates to fetch prayer times
  • Google AdMob - For serving ads
No personal data is collected or shared with third parties.

4. Data Retention

All data is processed locally on your device:

  • Location data - Used temporarily and not stored
  • Prayer times - Cached locally for offline use
  • Notification preferences - Stored on device

5. User Controls

You can:

  • Disable location services in app settings
  • Turn off individual prayer notifications
  • Revoke permissions via device settings
  • Clear all app data by uninstalling

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy and will notify users of significant changes.
